HOW TO ORDER

A step-by-step guide to placing custom orders, taking impressions and shipping them to us. Follow these steps to ensure a perfect fit and timely production.

Order Now Get an Impression Kit

This page summarises the classic process for ordering custom jewellery & grillz. It follows the same steps shown in the PDF you attached: select your product, take an impression, send the kit back, production & QC, then shipping. If you want anything changed or to preserve exact wording from the PDF, tell me which lines and I’ll match them verbatim. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

1

Choose your product

Browse categories (Grillz, Nail Jewelry, Earrings, etc.), pick size & finish. Use the product page to select metal, stones and any custom options.

Browse Products
2

Order an impression kit

For a perfect fit we use impressions. Order our kit — it contains putty, trays and detailed instructions. Kits normally ship worldwide within 2 business days.

Order Impression Kit
3

Take your impressions

Follow the included instructions: mix, seat the tray firmly, hold for the specified time. Rinse and label your impressions exactly as instructed to avoid delays.

  • Use a clean, well-lit area.
  • Follow the time & seating tips in the kit.
  • Label top/bottom and left/right when required.
4

Return the kit

Place your impressions and any forms into the prepaid envelope (if included) or follow our shipping address instructions. We recommend tracked shipping for international orders.

Include: order number, contact email, and any special notes (fit/tightness preferences).

5

Production & quality check

We fabricate to your specified materials, then hand-finish and inspect each piece. Production lead times are shown on product pages — rush options may be available.

We will email photos for approval if major custom work is requested.

6

Shipping & tracking

Once shipped you’ll receive tracking details. Delivery times depend on destination and shipping method — international customs may cause delays.

Contact Support

Care & aftercare

To keep your jewellery looking its best: avoid harsh chemicals, remove while showering or exercising, and clean with a soft cloth. For plated pieces, avoid frequent polishing.

  • Store in a dry place.
  • Polish gently with a soft cloth.
  • Contact us for repairs or resizing options.

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take?
Production + shipping depends on product & location — typical turnaround 2–6 weeks. Rush options available at checkout.
Can I change my order?
Minor changes are possible if production hasn't started. Contact us immediately with your order number.
What if my impression is bad?
We will inspect impressions on receipt — if unusable we’ll contact you with guidance for retake (may delay delivery).
